Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

This privacy policy describes how the SQAAF Folder Utility web application (developed using Google Apps Script) uses your information.

1. Information Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any external server or third-party service. All actions occur securely within your Google account environment.

2. Google Drive Access

The app requires access to your Google Drive to:

  • Create a folder structure called "SQAAF" with 128 standard subfolders.
  • Allow you to share a folder with others.
  • Allow you to clone a shared folder to your own Drive.

This access is limited to only what is necessary and happens with your explicit permission.

3. Data Usage

No data is collected, stored, or processed outside of your own Google account. All interactions with your Drive are handled by Google Apps Script and stay within your account's security boundaries.

4. Consent

By using this app, you consent to the limited use of your Google Drive only for the folder operations described above.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. All updates will be posted on this page.
